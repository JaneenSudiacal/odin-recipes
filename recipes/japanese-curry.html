Japanese Curry

Description

An easy, one pot recipe for japanese curry

If you can boil water, you can make this delicious curry!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Japanese Curry Cubes

Steps

  1. Chop chicken and veggies into bite sized pieces
  2. Saute onions in large pot until soft then add chicken
  3. Cook chicken until no longer pink and add potato and carrots. Saute for 5 minutes.
  4. Add water until everything is just covered
  5. Bring to boil and remove white foam
  6. Bring to simmer and cover with a lid for 15 minutes
  7. Add curry cubes and mix.
  8. Serve over hot rice!

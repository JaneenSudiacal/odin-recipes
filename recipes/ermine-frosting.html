Super Secret Frosting

Description

The best not-too-sweet frosting

Tastes like whipped cream but has the structure of buttercream!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • White Sugar
  • Warm Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Cook flour and sugar in saucepan, stirring constantly
  2. While whisking, slowly add warm milk. Cook until it becomes a thick custard texture
  3. Transfer mixture to bowl and cover with plastic wrap, pressing down on the surface
  4. Cool to room temperature
  5. Beat butter for 3 minutes or until it turns fluffy and pale yellow
  6. While beating butter, add flour mixture one tbsp at a time
  7. Add vanilla and salt and you're done!
